About

This is a teeny and simple webapp for choosing a random member of a group to give a presentation.

To add members to the team, click the blue bar.

To Remove members, click their name as it appears in one of the lists. You will be given the choice to remove them.

To select a member to give a presentation, click the large grey button in the middle. If you accept them as the presenter, they will automatically be recorded as having presented. If you want to manually change whether a member is recorded as presented/not presented, click their name in the list they appear in. Then, simply click the large checkbox and update. If the checkbox is selected, the member is stored as hasaving presented.

Occasionally, the page malfunctions and displays a JSON response instead of html. Simply navigate to /choose/ to remedy this.
